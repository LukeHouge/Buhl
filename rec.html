Parks and Recreation Board

Buhl's Parks and Recreation Board is responsible for the operation,maintenance and planning of the Buhl Parks and the various Recreation programs conducted by the City.

Buhl Parks and Recreation is managed by a 5 member volunteer board appointed by the Mayor and City Council.

Terms are for 3 years and require dedication, determination, a lot of energy, and a commitment to keeping Buhl a great place to live.

The current board welcomes community input and suggestions, and invites you to participate. Meetings are held the 2nd Monday of each month at the skating shack. Announcements are posted at the library, city hall, and post office. Event Notices are posted there as well as First National Bank and Short Stop.

Bring your suggestions to city hall or ask for board member phone numbers to contact them directly.
