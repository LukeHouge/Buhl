Boards and Commissions

The City of Buhl has various Boards and Commissions to advise the City Council on issues within their areas of operation.

  • Library Board meets on the third Thursday of each month at 6:00 p.m. at the Library.
  • Buhl Economic Development Authority meets on the last Wednesday of each month at 6:00 p.m. at City Hall.
  • Recreation Board meets on the second Monday of each month at 5:30 p.m. at the Warming House.
  • Long Range Planning and Zoning Commission meets on the second Tuesday of January, April, July, and October at 6:00 p.m. at City Hall.

Please contact City Hall at 218-258-3226 if you are interested in serving.
